Upside-Down Pizza Casserole

Discription

This Upside Down Pizza Casserole is filled with 3 meats, vegetables, and gooey cheese! Top with homemade or store bought pizza dough or even biscuits for the ultimate weeknight meal.

This is basically the easiest way to make a pizza! It doesn’t have to look pretty so don’t worry about your pizza dough skills (you can even cheat and use store bought if you prefer!)

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef

  • 1 (15 ounce) can pizza sauce

  • 1 (4 ounce) can sliced mushrooms, drained (Optional)

  • 1/4 cup sliced pitted ripe olives

  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheesse, more to taste

  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated biscuit dough

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).

  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and dicard grease. Stir in sauce, mushrooms, and olives. Cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes more. Transer mixture to a 2-quart rectangular baking dish. Sprinkle mozzerella cheese on top Casserole.

  3. Flatten each biscuit with your hands and arrange on top of the cheese.

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are golden, 15 to 17 minutes.
